● About

More than a decade
of engineering excellence.

Born in Brazil, crafting software for the world.

I'm Rafael, a Senior Software Engineer and Architect based in Porto Alegre, Brazil. For over 20 years, I've been obsessing over how to build distributed systems that are resilient, scalable, and — above all — reliable under pressure.

My career spans fintech, gaming, and enterprise software. From early monoliths to event-driven microservices running on AWS, I've seen architectures evolve and learned that every great system is a series of deliberate trade-offs.

Lately, I've been diving deep into AI tooling — not as a hype-driven experiment, but as another leverage point in the engineer's toolkit.

"Building scalable systems is an exercise in trade-offs. Great engineers don't chase perfection — they optimize for outcomes."
